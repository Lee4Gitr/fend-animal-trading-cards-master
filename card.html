Skag

Skag tongues grow with age.

  • Name Origin: Skags are named after a derogatory term for a young biker who followed Nightrider in Mad Max
  • Planet Origin: Pandora
  • Types: Pup, Whelp, Adult, Spitter, Elder, Alpha, and many others
  • Weakness: Fire

Skags are large, dog-like creatures, easily identified by their strong bony armor plates, aggressive behavior, and distinctive three-jawed mouths.
